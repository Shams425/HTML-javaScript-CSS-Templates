Ajman Public Health Program

Program Scope

The Ajman Public Health Program (APHP) is a multi-year, comprehensive initiative to safeguard public health in the Emirate of Ajman. The scope of the APHP will include gyms, salons, barber shops and clubs, and will begin with all food businesses.

The APHP is based on international best practice for training, examinations, management systems, audit and certification, as well as program management.
