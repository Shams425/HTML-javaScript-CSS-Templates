TSI Training

As part of our government programs, we develop and run training courses.

With our team of education specialists, we offer a fresh and highly effective approach to training, with incredible feedback from government and industry clients. We can also provide UK accredited, internationally recognised qualifications.

Courses cover topics such as management and leadership, food safety management, quality management, risk management, train-the-trainer, and organisational culture.

an expert team

Three of our TSI Directors are qualified teachers (PgCert / Masters in Education) with PhDs. They have led courses at Colleges and Universities in the UK and UAE, and have widely presented and published their work on teaching and education.

The TSI approach to teaching and education is learner-centred, with a focus on practical behavioural change as well as the provision of knowledge and understanding. Training courses are carefully designed to provide maximum impact for the learner and their organisation.

Accredited uk courses

TSI are proud to partner with FutureQuals to deliver UK accredited, internationally recognised courses. We provide a diverse range of options, from short courses to full Management Diplomas.
